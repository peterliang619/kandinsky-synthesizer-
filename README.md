# Kandinsky Synthesizer

A creative synthesis tool inspired by Wassily Kandinsky's abstract art principles.

## Creative Thinking Methodology: SCAMPER

This project embraces the SCAMPER creative thinking framework to explore and transform sound, visuals, and concepts. SCAMPER is a powerful brainstorming technique that encourages innovation through seven key approaches:

- Who else could be involved?
- What materials can replace existing ones?
- What other processes could achieve the same result?

*Example: Replace sine waves with square waves, substitute visual colors with sound frequencies*

- What can be combined to create something new?
- Can we merge different sounds or visual elements?

- What happens when we unite contradictory elements?

*Example: Combine audio synthesis with visual generation, merge multiple sound sources*

*Example: Adapt visual art principles to sound design, reshape audio curves for visual output*

*Example: Use audio algorithms for visual generation, apply rhythm patterns to color transitions*

## Getting Started

Explore the synthesizer using these creative principles to discover new sounds, visuals, and combinations. Experiment freely and let creativity guide your process!

## Project Philosophy

Like Kandinsky's approach to abstract art, this synthesizer encourages breaking traditional boundaries and exploring the synesthetic relationship between sound and vision. Use SCAMPER to continuously innovate and discover new creative possibilities.
